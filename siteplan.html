Site name

Steep Canyon Adventure Tours

Pourpose

The pourpose of this site is to provide information on white water rafting tours. Users will be able to easily understand the company's devotion to safe but fun travel on the river. The user will be able to schedule tours on the river as well as have the option to purchase souvinier photos from past rafting trips.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site would be a user scheduling white water rafting tours for a group. Different tour lengths will be offered to allow the user to find the best option for their budget. Other options should be made for those users looking to join an open tour group. The site will also help customers that have previously taken a tour to find photos of their adventure.

Logo

Color Scheme

The color scheme will use a natural outdoor color pallet. Headers and footers will be a dusty green/brown and the body will be a tan.

Typography

The site will be using the Stick No Bills font from Google fonts for the header and roboto for the body
