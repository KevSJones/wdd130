We run rivers to relax.

Winding through canyons in in some of the most beautiful land anywhere, the river flows towards the mighty Appalachian trail. High grey stone cliffs, beautiful forest scenery, and great rapids have made this run famous. This trip is relaxing and boasts outstanding scenery, interesting geologic formations, evidence of prehistoric and historic human activity, and great whitewater opportunities.

We run whitewater for the rush!

Let's not forget the rapids! They range from class I-IV. Our route is one of the longest undammed rivers in the United States. As a free flowing river the water is higher, faster, and colder in the early summer and lower, slower, and warmer in the late summer. There are good rapids all summer...but families with children who like to swim tend to appreciate the late summer's slower, warmer water more.

Come run with us!
