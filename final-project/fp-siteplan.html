Final Project Site Plan

Site Purpose:

There is desperate need of a good resource to find information on junior cycling (kids ages 8-17). The only other website that I have with local information is specifically for Indiana's high school mountain bike league and whenever I go to that site I leave with more questions and frustration than I started. It would be nice if there was one place that I could go to find junior races near me as well as a place where I could go to learn more about all types of cycling and how to get into the sport. There is a large divide between the amount of kids getting into cycling compared with other sports and I feel that the reason is because there is a lack of information and a disconnect on how to get started. My goal would be to provide information to athletes starting out as well as be the go-to resource for everyone to find out when and where the next junior race will be.

Target Audience

The intended audience will be junior bike racers and parents looking to find information on upcoming races. The site will have a "Find A Race Near Me" section. As well as a section where people can view results on recent races. Another reason for the site would be a place of information on how kids can get involved in racing and/or training. That information will include an area where the user can find used equipment for a good price. And a place where people can donate bikes and gear for other junior cyclist to use.

Color Scheme and Typography

Right now I am leaning towards red blue and black but will play around with the colors a bit this next week. I liked the font I used for my rafting website but I might try something with sharper corners for this one. These will be worked on more in the morning when I'm not as tired and cranky.
